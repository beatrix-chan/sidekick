# SideKick

An app made for women by women that accelerates student life within the same university.

## Overview

**Problem**<br />
Female students often waste time and feel isolated because help is scattered across group chats, societies, and informal networks. It’s hard to quickly find **trusted** advice on modules, admin processes, placements/CVs, or simply “how do I do X on campus?”—and cold-DM networking can feel unsafe or awkward.

Also, late-night campus routines can feel unsafe alone, and it’s hard to quickly find a trusted walking partner.

**Solution**

1. Fast Help (Q&A + Requests)
   - Ask/get answers by category
   - Created purpose-based help requests (CV review, module help, post-grad planning)
2. Safe Walk (Nearby Match + PIN Verify + Live Progress)
   - Requestion a walking partner for a campus route (Building A &rarr; Building B, leaving now / in 10 minutes, in pair)
   - Get matched with verified students
   - Privacy-first: users are shown as randomly generated nicknames instead of real names.
   - Meet-up safety: each Safe Walk session generates a unique PIN. When matched users meet, they exchange PINs and confirm in-app before the walk is considered "verified".
